Payout Peak

Experience a mountaineering adventure in Payout Peak! Collect gems to win a prize. Collect Up Arrow symbols to climb higher on the mountain. But watch out! The Rockfall symbols might take you back down the mountain. But the good news is that if you collect 4 Up Arrow or 4 Rockfall symbols, you still win a prize. Land on a cave and trigger one of two bonus games—or you might even win a prize instantly. The further you climb the mountain the higher the prizes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Payout Peak is to collect symbols or win a prize instantly.

Press CLIMB to advance the climber up the mountain. Or press AUTO CLIMB to play the game automatically to completion. Press the Map to see an overview of the mountain.

When the climber lands on an Up Arrow symbol, the symbol is collected and the climber advances up the mountain. When the climber lands on a Rockfall symbol, the symbol is collected and the climber falls down the mountain. Collect 4 Up Arrow symbols or 4 Rockfall symbols to win a prize.

When the climber lands on a Question Mark symbol, a gem, an Up Arrow, a Rockfall, an extra climb, or an instant prize is revealed. Up Arrow symbols and Rockfall symbols revealed by the Question Mark do not move the climber up or down.

When the climber lands on an Extra Climb symbol, an extra climb is awarded.

Collect a gem when the climber lands on a gem symbol. Collect 4 or more gems to win a prize.

Trigger one of two bonus games when the climber lands on the Cave symbol. Pick a stone or navigate using the compass to win a prize or a collection symbol.

Play ends when no climbs remain and any bonus games have been played. A message appears indicating the game’s outcome.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
